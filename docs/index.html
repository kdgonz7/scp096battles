SCP-096 Battles

The Ultimate 1v1 Showdown

Face your fears or become the nightmare. In SCP-096 Battles, one player hunts as the unstoppable SCP-096 while the other fights for survival as an armed human. Earn points, upgrade your abilities, and dominate the arena.

Intense 1v1 Combat

Experience heart-pounding, asymmetrical battles where strategy and reflexes are key. Whether you're the hunter or the hunted, every match is a unique challenge. Adapt your tactics to outsmart your opponent in close-quarters maps designed for confrontation.

Become the Nightmare: SCP-096

Play as the infamous "Shy Guy". Initially passive, unleash unstoppable rage when provoked. Utilize incredible speed, devastating melee attacks, and unique abilities to hunt down the human relentlessly. Master the rage mechanic to become an inescapable force.

Fight for Survival: The Human

As the human, your survival depends on firepower and cunning. Access a diverse arsenal of Garry's Mod weapons to fight back against SCP-096. Use the environment to your advantage, manage your ammo, and land critical shots before it's too late. Can you keep your cool and survive the encounter?

Earn Points & Upgrade

Victory earns points! Use your hard-earned points between rounds to purchase powerful upgrades. Enhance SCP-096's abilities (like faster rage activation or increased health) or unlock better weapons and equipment for the human. Customize your loadout to match your playstyle.
